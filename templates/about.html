About 'Dog Breed Detection'

Have you ever wanted to know the breed of your dog? Don't pay for an expensive DNA test, use Dog Breed Detection!

This website uses a pre-trained machine learning model called VG-99 (CNN) to transcribe dog images and predict your dog breed.

Easy to use, two steps!

Step 1: Upload a picture of your pooch.

Step 2: Click 'Create Chart' to see your results!
